Julia Brumley

Qualifications

Graduate Diploma of Psychology – Swinburne University
Graduate Diploma of Counselling and Human Services – LaTrobe University
Bachelor of Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences (Anthropology major) – LaTrobe University

For over 16 years Julia has been a practicing psychotherapist and counsellor treating individuals, couples, young people and families. She has post graduate degrees in both counselling and psychology.

Julia commenced her career in perinatal mental health, providing counselling support to women, men and families experiencing post and ante natal depression and adjusting to parenting. She practiced in this field for 10 years and was also a senior clinician leading a team of multi-disciplined counselling clinicians.

She has also worked for 14 years as a senior clinician in alcohol and other drug and gambling addiction settings.

With practice rooms in Blairgowrie and available via telehealth if preferred or more practical, Julia provides counselling and psychotherapy for a range of emotional difficulties and problem behaviours and has completed studies in couples counselling; Family therapy;

Single session therapy; CBT; Motivational interviewing and enhancement and ACT (acceptance and commitment therapy).

She is warm and empathetic and uses humanistic; psychodynamic and neoanalytic frameworks to her clinical work.
