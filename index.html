My approach

As a practicing counsellor and psychotherapist I have over 15 years experience treating individuals, couples, adolescents and families. I assist clients seeking treatment for depression and anxiety, trauma, substance use and addiction and couples seeking relationship counselling.

I bring authentic compassion, empathy and insight into every client interaction, which is essential to beginning the healing of any of these issues. What sets me apart is a combination of my warmth, insight and practicality, that is informed by studies in both Counselling and Psychology as well as real life experience.
