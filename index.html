I provide quality counselling and psychotherapy for individuals and families on the Mornington Peninsula and surrounding areas.
Feel free to reach out to discuss how we can work together.

My approach

As a practicing counsellor and psychotherapist I have 16 years experience treating individuals, couples, adolescents and families. I assist clients seeking treatment for depression and anxiety, trauma, substance use and addiction and couples seeking relationship counselling.

I bring authentic compassion, empathy and insight into every client interaction, which is essential to beginning the healing of any of these issues. What sets me apart from the average therapist is a combination of my warmth, insight and practicality, that is informed by studies in both Counselling and Psychology as well as real life experience.
